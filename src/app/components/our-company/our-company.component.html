About Us

International Halal AI Research and Training Centre (INHART-AI) is the world’s pioneering institution at the intersection of Halal compliance, Artificial Intelligence, and global innovation. Headquartered in Uzbekistan, INHART-AI leads the transformation of the Halal ecosystem through cutting-edge research, advanced AI integration, and strategic capacity-building programs tailored for the Halal industry worldwide.

We empower governments, certification bodies, corporations, and entrepreneurs to elevate their Halal standards by leveraging the latest in AI-driven technologies With a global vision and regional focus, we are proud to be the first and only Halal AI institution, unlocking new commercial frontiers and investment opportunities in food, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, finance, tourism, logistics, medical device and digital services. Our training programs and consultancy services are designed to future-proof Halal industries and accelerate digital transformation in compliance with international Shariah standards. At INHART-AI, we are not just keeping pace with innovation—we are defining the future of Halal.
